What we'd suggest for each of these practices next.

Open Door Dental Group — with 2 specialists already embedded, the next step is a Denterra team lead. One senior specialist standardizes verification SOPs across Central Point, Medford, and Grants Pass and unlocks a third location-wide specialist without adding in-office headcount.
