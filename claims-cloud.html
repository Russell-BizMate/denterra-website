Why We Built It

Dental admin software hasn't kept up with dental practices.

Our specialists were spending half their day clicking through carrier portals, copying data between tabs, and re-keying the same information into three systems. We knew AI could change that — but no one was building it for dental.

So we built Claims Cloud ourselves. It sits alongside your practice management software and does the tedious parts automatically, so our specialists can focus on judgment calls, edge cases, and patient conversations.

Claims Cloud isn't sold separately. It's the reason every Denterra placement gets more done per hour.

What It Does

Built for the exact work dental admin actually does.

Automated Eligibility & Benefits

Pulls eligibility, benefits, frequency limits, and deductibles from the top 10 carrier portals — before the patient arrives. No more manual lookups.

Pre-Submission Claim Review

AI scans every claim for missing attachments, wrong codes, coordination-of-benefits errors, and carrier-specific gotchas — before you hit submit.

AI-Drafted Collections Outreach

Writes first-pass patient and carrier follow-ups trained on your practice's voice. Specialists review and send — drafting time cut by 80%.

Live Specialist Dashboards

See every verifier's day at a glance — queue, aging, productivity, bottlenecks. No more "what did we do today?" on Fridays.

Practice Management Sync

Two-way sync with Dentrix, Eaglesoft, and Open Dental. Updates write back to your system automatically — no double entry.

HIPAA-Compliant by Design

Encrypted end-to-end, audit-logged, BAA-ready. Built with the same compliance posture your practice holds itself to.
