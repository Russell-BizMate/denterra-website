Scaling With Denterra

A process built for lasting results, not just a quick placement.

Six steps. Every candidate. Every practice.

1

We find and certify the right people.

We recruit internationally for motivated professionals with foundational IV experience and a proven remote-work track record, then put them through our dental-specific certification program. Character matters as much as credentials.

2

We prep them for your world.

Before any introduction, we prepare every candidate for U.S. dental office norms, communication expectations, and remote-work protocols — so the transition is smooth from day one.

3

We learn your practice — deeply.

A dedicated discovery session to understand your long-term goals, RCM workflows, software stack, and how you want to build your team over time.

4

We match and you choose.

We present your best-fit candidate options. You interview the ones you want and make the final call. No pressure, no surprises.

5

We integrate — technically and culturally.

Phone lines, practice management access, secure comms. Plus a proven process for helping dental offices build real connection with their international team members.

6

We stay in it with you.

Ongoing KPI tracking, performance management, and development support. A long-term partnership, not a one-time transaction.
