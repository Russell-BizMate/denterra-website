Pricing FAQ

What practices ask us.

Is $15 the only cost?
Yes. $15/hour all-in covers the team member's pay, our recruiting and skill verification, their equipment and secure workstation tools, payroll/tax handling on our side, and ongoing performance management. No placement fees. No setup fees. No surprise overages.
Do you offer part-time?
No — we place full-time only. We've found part-time doesn't give a practice enough room to truly involve someone in the culture or help them build a real career with you. Full-time is what makes the long-term investment, and the loyalty that comes with it, actually work.
How is billing handled?
You pay for the first two weeks up front when you get started. After that, every two weeks we run a standard ACH charge for the previous two weeks your team member worked. No placement fees, no long-term contract.
What happens if the fit isn't right?
We re-match at no additional cost. Our integration process is built to catch fit issues in the first 30 days — and we stay close past that.
What if I want to build a larger team?
If you're planning to build a team of more than three people, we can work on some potential special pricing. Bring it up on your discovery call.
