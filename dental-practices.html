FAQ

Questions practice owners ask us.

How long until someone is working in my practice?
Most practices have a team member seated and trained within 2–3 weeks from discovery call. For urgent seats, we can accelerate to 7–10 days.
Are they employees or contractors?
Denterra team members are international contractors fully managed by us. You get a long-term team member without the W-2 overhead, payroll tax burden, or HR complexity. We handle pay, benefits, equipment, and performance management on our side.
Which practice management systems do you support?
Every Denterra team member is introduced to Dentrix, Eaglesoft, and Open Dental, and typically has more extensive experience with at least one. They also work across most major U.S. carrier portals.
Is this HIPAA-compliant?
Yes. Every team member completes our HIPAA training and certification before placement. We use secure workstation tooling, role-based access controls, and BAAs are signed with every practice partner before a team member starts.
What happens if the fit isn't right?
We re-match at no additional cost. Our integration process is built to catch fit issues in the first 30 days — and we stay in it with you well past that, with consistent monthly check-ins to make sure things keep moving in the right direction.
How is pricing structured?
Flat per-hour pricing — $16/hour for full-time — tied to the role. No placement fees, no surprise overages. We'll walk you through the exact numbers on your discovery call.
