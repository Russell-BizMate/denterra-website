Scaling With Denterra

A process built for lasting results, not just a quick placement.

1

We find and certify the right people.

We recruit internationally for motivated professionals with foundational IV experience and a proven remote-work track record. Every candidate goes through our dental-specific certification program — HIPAA training, dental terminology, procedure codes, insurance portals, and practice management software — so they arrive ready, not learning on the job. Character matters as much as credentials.

2

We prep them for your world.

Before any introduction, we prepare every candidate for U.S. dental office norms, communication expectations, and remote-work protocols — so the transition into your team is smooth and professional from day one.

3

We learn your practice — deeply.

A dedicated discovery session to understand your long-term goals, RCM workflows, software stack, and how you want to build your team over time. We establish your point of contact and align on exactly what success looks like for your office.

4

We match and you choose.

Based on everything we've learned about your practice, we present your best-fit candidate options. You interview the ones you want and make the final call. No pressure, no surprises.

5

We integrate them — technically and culturally.

We help handle the full tech setup: phone lines, practice management software access, and secure communication systems. And we have a proven process for helping dental offices build real connection and culture with their international team members.

6

We stay in it with you.

After placement, we follow up consistently — tracking KPIs, managing performance, and supporting your team member's ongoing development as they grow deeper into your RCM process. This is a long-term partnership, not a one-time transaction.

FAQ

Questions practice owners ask us.

How long until someone is working in my practice?

Most practices have a specialist seated and trained within 2–3 weeks from discovery call. For urgent seats, we can accelerate to 7–10 days.

Are they employees or contractors?

Denterra specialists are engaged through us — you don't handle international payroll, compliance, or HR. They work exclusively with your practice and integrate like a team member.

Which practice management systems do you support?

Dentrix, Eaglesoft, and Open Dental out of the box. We've also placed specialists on Curve, Denticon, and tab32.

Is this HIPAA-compliant?

Yes. Every specialist is HIPAA-trained and certified before placement. We use secure, encrypted communication systems and follow BAA-ready workflows.

What happens if the fit isn't right?

We replace quickly — at no extra charge inside the guarantee window. In practice, our screening catches most issues before placement.

How is pricing structured?

Flat monthly per-seat pricing based on role and hours. No per-claim fees, no per-verification charges, no surprises. We'll walk you through exact numbers on the discovery call.
